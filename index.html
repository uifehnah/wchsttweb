ROBOTIC SNOW REMOVAL

Snowbotix provides a comprehensive automated robotics solution for on-demand, consistent and cost-effective snow and ice removal in sidewalks, parking lots, bike lanes, and driveways to enable unrestricted mobility and accessibility. Snowbotix is a socially aware, fully electric smart robot capable of safely navigating the preplanned routes while avoiding obstacles for clearing snow/ice along its path and piling in designated spots. Snowbotix can perceive its environment with the highest level of situational awareness to optimally plan the safest, energy-efficient, and obstacle-free path to clear snow over an area, return to the charging station when the task is completed. Snowbotix is built as durable tracked multi-functional robots for all-terrain (snow-covered/bare pavement, grass, gravel, curbs), all-season (snow, wet, dry), and all environment (day, night, bright, dark) operations. Snowbotix is pre-programmed for autonomous operation or onboard intelligent navigation aided teleoperation via our cloud-based planning, monitoring, and execution service. Snowbotix is integrated with state-of-the-art perception, path planning, obstacle avoidance navigation, and control components to enable SAFE, RELIABLE snow plowing routines in a complex environment.
